How to choose the right chandelier for your Nairobi home

Start with room size: compact rooms work best with smaller chandeliers, while open dining areas can carry wider designs. Keep the fixture centered over the table or key furniture zone for visual balance.

For ceiling height, choose shorter drops in apartments and longer drops in higher spaces. Warm white bulbs create a cozy mood, while cool white gives a sharper modern look.
