How to choose the right pendant lights for your Nairobi home

For dining tables, keep pendant drops low enough for ambience but high enough for clear sightlines. Over kitchen islands, space fixtures evenly to avoid bright and dark patches.

Choose natural materials for warm interiors and metal or glass for modern spaces. Match bulb tone to the mood you want in the room.
