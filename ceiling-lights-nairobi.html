How to choose the right ceiling lights for your Nairobi home

For low ceilings, use flush or semi-flush fittings that keep visual space open. In larger rooms, choose wider fixtures with better beam spread to avoid dark corners.

Use warm white for bedrooms and cool white for kitchens and work areas. Always match fitting size to room dimensions for a balanced look.
